**Knowledge-Based Inference for Real-Time Decisioning**



## Overview

ZeroTrain.ai delivers a **complete, knowledge-based AI ecosystem** designed for:

- Real-time decisioning
- Full explainability
- Zero training requirements

Unlike traditional AI systems, ZeroTrain operates using:

> **Deterministic symbolic reasoning — not statistical models**
